Published Works

Opinion Piece on NFL Player Safety

  • I wrote an opinion piece on the state of player safety in the NFL - linking it to Aaron Rodgers' Achilles tear as a concrete consequence of neglecting player needs. Player injuries happen far too often in the NFL, and while there are easy methods to lessen occurrences, the monetary consequence of those paths prevents billion-dollar industries from going down them.

Profile Story on a Local Chainsaw Artist

  • I interviewed a local chainsaw artist and wrote a profile story on him, gathering his life story and getting it published in the Greater Olney News. I created this piece for my news writing class - but had a blast doing it. The chainsaw artist had an incredibly interesting story to tell.

Olney Panhandling Story
